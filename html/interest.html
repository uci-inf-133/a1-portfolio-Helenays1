Personal Interests

Helena has two desires on her mind all the time: video games and a little treat (mostly in the form of milk tea). These two inanimate passions are what keep her through the sunshines and rainstroms of her college journey. Let's take a closer look at what exactly make Helena click.

Milk Tea

Helena loves floral and fruity milk teas. Although creamy is considered a rich taste by most, Helena finds it too heavy and strong. Add-ons such as boba and jelly are chosen carefully for most orders as they might stir the flavor of the tea itself.

Video Games

Fast-paced combat action gives Helena the most dopamine and adrenaline.

fig 1. Ultrakill game cover art features a robotic character holding 2 weapons.

Ultrakill

Ultrakill is Helena's favorite game of all times. It's a low-res fast-paced flashy game of a robot with guns. The robot, V1, can parry almost any attack and has an ability where it shots at a coin and have its bullet ricochet right into the enemy. Ultrakill also has a deep horrific lore involving humanity, religion, and existentialism.

Hollow Knight Silksong

Hollow Knight Silksong is another of Helena's favorite due to its fast-paced combat. Helena loves the first Hollow Knight game, and the sequal only made her love the series more. The world building, lore, art, and music are awe-inspring. Helena listens to Hollow Knight and Silksong soundtracks when she needs to focus on work.

fig 2. Hollow Knight Silksong game cover art features a bug-like character.

fig 3. Overwatch 2 art features character Hanzo with and a bow.

Overwatch 2

Overwatch 2 is a team-based shooter game that Helena plays casually with friends. Overwatch 2 is very attention intensive, and Helena can lose herself in the game for hours. Currently, she is enjoying playing as Hanzo, a bow-wielding character.
